## Items Marked UNVERIFIED

The following items could not be verified from official Etsy sources or reliable cross-references:

1. **Exact freshness boost duration** — official Etsy documentation does not publish a specific number of hours/days. Multiple third-party sources say "hours to a few days." Treat as approximate. Mark: UNVERIFIED — needs follow-up if a precise window is required.
2. **Lifestyle imagery 5x CTR claim** — cited by listybox.com without a primary source. The directional claim (lifestyle > plain mockup for CTR) is credible; the "5x" figure is unverified. Mark: UNVERIFIED — treat directionally but don't use as a hard number.
3. **Conversion rate benchmarks (1-3% for POD)** — approximate range from third-party analytics guides, not official Etsy data. Mark: UNVERIFIED — useful as a rough calibration, not a guarantee.
4. **Processing time as a direct ranking factor** — multiple third-party sources describe it as a signal; official Etsy documentation links it to Star Seller and Purchase Protection but does not explicitly state it boosts search ranking directly. Mark: UNVERIFIED — directionally credible.

## Follow-up Recommendations

1. Fetch the Etsy Seller Handbook "Anatomy of a Well-Crafted Listing" article (https://www.etsy.com/seller-handbook/article/1347574487014) when Etsy fixes 403 blocks — it likely contains official photo type and count guidance.
2. Verify conversion rate benchmarks against your own shop data once you have 90+ days of Stats history.
3. Review Etsy's "How Etsy Search Works" article annually (https://www.etsy.com/seller-handbook/article/375461474487) for algorithm updates.
4. Re-run eHunt research for each new listing — keyword competition scores change; tags that were low-competition 3 months ago may not be today.
