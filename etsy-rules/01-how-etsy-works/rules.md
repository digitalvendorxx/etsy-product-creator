# 01 — How Etsy Works: Operating Rules

## 1. TL;DR

- Etsy charges a $0.20 listing fee + 6.5% transaction fee + payment processing fee on every sale. All three stack.
- Search ranking is driven by relevance (title/tags match) + engagement signals (clicks, favorites, dwell time, purchases) + shop quality. Free shipping to US buyers improves placement for US domestic listings.
- Star Seller badge is re-evaluated on the 1st of every month; requires 95% message response rate within 24h, 95% on-time shipping with tracking, 4.8+ rating average, 5+ orders totaling $300+ in the prior 3 months.
- Purchase Protection (eff. 2026-05-07): Etsy covers buyer refunds up to $250 USD when seller meets eligibility criteria — ship on time with tracking, respond to Help with Order messages within 48h, listing matches item sent.
- Offsite Ads are mandatory for shops over $10,000/year in sales (cannot opt out); fee is 12%. Shops under $10,000 can opt out; fee if enrolled is 15%. Attribution window is 30 days.

## 3. Search Algorithm

Ranking factors in approximate priority order (verified 2026-04-27 from official Seller Handbook + search ranking disclosures):

1. **Query relevance** — Title, tags, attributes, and description must match the buyer's search terms. Exact phrase matches in title and first few tags carry the most weight.
2. **Listing quality score / engagement** — Combination of click-through rate, favorites, add-to-cart, and purchases. Also includes *dwell time* (how long a buyer stays on the listing page after clicking — confirmed in Q1 2025 investor call). A bounce signals poor relevance.
3. **Recency boost** — New listings get a short temporary boost (hours to a few days) so Etsy can gather engagement data. Renewed listings get a smaller boost. Renewing is not a reliable long-term strategy.
4. **Shipping price (US domestic only)** — Free shipping or shipping under $6 to US buyers improves placement in US search results. Offering a Free Shipping Guarantee ($35+ orders ship free to US) provides priority placement. Does not affect international results.
5. **Shop quality / customer experience** — Average review rating, message response rate, on-time shipping rate, and case rate from the past 3 months all factor in. Poor customer service metrics lower ranking.
6. **Listing quality signals** — Images under 2,000px wide are flagged. Having a stated return policy (even "no returns") improves placement. Completed attributes (size, color, material) help relevance matching.
7. **Personalization** — Etsy personalizes results per buyer based on their browsing/purchase history. Two buyers see different result orders for the same query.
8. **Market / recency context** — Etsy weighs context like seasonality and trending topics.

**Practical rules:**
- Put the most important keyword phrase in the first 3 words of the title.
- Do not repeat the same keyword in more than 30-40% of tags (Etsy warns about this in title tips).
- Tags are 13 max, up to 20 characters each; use all 13.
- Attributes (size, color) are additional ranking signals — always fill them.

## 4. Star Seller

### Eligibility criteria (all must be met in the 3-month review period)

| Metric | Threshold |
|---|---|
| Message response rate | >= 95% of first messages replied within 24h |
| On-time shipping + tracking | >= 95% of orders shipped on time with tracking OR Etsy shipping label purchased |
| Average review rating | >= 4.8 stars |
| Minimum order count | >= 5 orders totaling >= $300 (before shipping and taxes) |
| Account age | Must have first sale at least 90 days ago |
| Policy compliance | Shop must comply with all Etsy policies |

### Review and award schedule

- Eligibility re-evaluated on **the 1st of every month**, based on the prior 3 calendar months of activity.
- Badge appears within 24 hours of the 1st if criteria are met.
- Badge is removed on the 1st of any month criteria are no longer met.
- If badge is removed, a banner notification in the Star Seller dashboard explains the reason.

### What Star Seller affects

- Visible badge on shop and listing pages, visible in search results.
- May improve buyer trust and conversion rate.
- Does **not** directly boost search ranking as a standalone signal, but the underlying metrics (response rate, review rating, shipping) do feed into shop quality ranking signals.

## 5. Purchase Protection (effective 2026-05-07)

### Coverage

- Etsy covers buyer refunds up to **$250 USD** (including shipping and taxes collected on platform) per eligible order.
- Covers: item never arrives, item arrives late (7+ days after estimated delivery date), item significantly not as described.
- Damaged items: generally NOT covered, but Etsy may cover a seller's **first damaged-item case per calendar year**.
- Buyers can open a case up to **30 days after the estimated delivery date** (new window; aligns with industry standard).

### Seller eligibility requirements (all must be met)

1. Order shipped on time, within stated processing time.
2. Tracking provided OR Etsy shipping label purchased (where available).
3. Seller meets Etsy's minimum Customer Service Standards.
4. Seller responds to the buyer's Help with Order message within **48 hours** (automated vacation-mode auto-replies count).
5. Item matches listing description.
6. Shop is in good standing.

### What "late delivery" means (2026-05-07 definition)

An order is considered late if it arrives **7 or more days** after the estimated delivery date shown at checkout.

Prior to this policy, "late" was defined as 1 day after the EDD. The 7-day buffer was introduced in the May 2026 update.

### Exclusions

- Carrier delays due to force majeure (strike, severe weather, war, civil unrest) — at Etsy's discretion.
- Orders where buyer delayed or refused to pay customs charges owed on delivery.
- Damaged items (with exception noted above for first case/year).

### 2026 enforcement changes

- Etsy nearly doubled the number of human reviewers for complex cases.
- Complex cases now receive additional human review for both sides.

## 6. Listing Rules

### What can be listed (4 categories per Creativity Standards, updated June 10, 2025)

| Category | Definition | Key rule |
|---|---|---|
| Made by a Seller | Physical items handmade by you or made with a computerized tool (3D printer, CNC, Cricut, laser cutter) | Must be based on **seller's original design** — templates by others no longer allowed (June 2025 change) |
| Designed by a Seller | Seller's original designs produced by a third party (POD) or sold as digital download | Design must be seller's original work |
| Handpicked by a Seller | Vintage items (20+ years old), items from nature, curated collections | Vintage is the primary use case; digital scans of vintage content **removed from allowed items** (June 2025 change) |
| Sourced by a Seller | Craft supplies — tools/materials whose primary purpose is creating new items | Holiday decor and party favors **removed** from this category (June 2025 change) |

### Prohibited items (key categories)

- Recalled products (by government or manufacturer).
- Guns, blatant weapons (even vintage).
- Alcohol, tobacco, drugs, drug paraphernalia, medical drugs.
- Items that promote, support, or glorify hatred or discrimination.
- Counterfeit goods or items infringing third-party IP.
- Items from nature not incorporated into a new item (e.g., reselling unmodified raw materials from nature is now explicitly prohibited).
- Any item Etsy deems to violate its Prohibited Items Policy.

### IP (Intellectual Property) rules

- Etsy acts under DMCA: valid takedown notices result in **immediate listing deactivation** with no prior warning to seller.
- Sellers can file a DMCA counter notice; removed listing may be restored 10 business days after counter notice is processed, unless copyright owner seeks a court order.
- Repeat infringement = account termination.

### Listing quality rules (practical)

- Images should be at least 2,000px wide (Etsy flags sub-standard images).
- Must have a stated return policy (even "no returns") — affects search placement.
- Listing title guidance: Etsy recommends <= 14 words, no flagged keyword repeats, move subjective adjectives to description.
- Always complete all relevant attributes (size, color, material, occasion).

### June 2025 Creativity Standards — key changes

- 3D printing / CNC / Cricut items must now use **seller's original designs** only; template-based items no longer qualify as "Made by a Seller."
- Digital scans of vintage content (photographs, books, patterns) are no longer explicitly allowed under the "Handpicked" category — listings of this type may be removed.
- Natural items (shells, wood, etc.) sold without modification now prohibited.
- Party favors and holiday decor removed from "Sourced by a Seller."

## 7. Shipping Rules

### Shipping profile types

- **Fixed price** — You set a flat shipping cost per item or per order. Most common for handmade items where weight/size is predictable.
- **Calculated shipping** — Cost calculated in real time based on carrier rates, destination, and parcel dimensions. **Note:** As of March 3, 2025, Etsy no longer allows handling fees on calculated shipping profiles.
- **Free shipping** — Set at $0 for specific regions or all destinations. Can be applied per listing or via shop-wide guarantee.

### Free Shipping Guarantee (US)

- A shop-level setting in Shop Manager.
- Automatically applies free shipping to all orders totaling **$35 USD or more** shipping within or to the US.
- Auto-applies to new listings over $35 USD when the guarantee is active.
- Does not affect international shipping prices.
- Benefit: **priority placement in US search results** and visible "Free shipping" badge on listings.

### Processing time and estimated delivery date (EDD)

- EDD shown at checkout = your stated processing time + carrier transit time.
- Sellers must ship within their stated processing time to be eligible for Star Seller and Purchase Protection.
- Shipping with tracking is required for Purchase Protection coverage.

### Late delivery (post 2026-05-07 definition)

Order is "late" if it arrives 7 or more days after the EDD shown at checkout.

### Shipping labels via Etsy

- Etsy offers discounted shipping labels through its partnerships with USPS, FedEx, UPS (US) and other carriers in select countries.
- Purchasing an Etsy label counts as providing tracking for Star Seller and Purchase Protection purposes.

## 8. Reviews & Messaging

### Reviews

**What sellers CAN do:**
- Send a follow-up message thanking the buyer after delivery.
- Include a card in packaging asking for honest feedback.
- Respond publicly to any review (response is visible to all visitors).

**What sellers CANNOT do:**
- Pressure a buyer to change or remove a negative review.
- Offer incentives (discounts, refunds, gifts) conditioned on receiving a positive review.
- Leave fake reviews or purchase from a competitor to leave negative reviews.
- Create a separate buyer account to upvote negative reviews of a competitor.

**Removal:** Etsy cannot remove reviews that comply with its policies, even if the seller disputes the buyer's opinion. Reviews that contain prohibited content (personal info, profanity, discriminatory language) can be flagged for removal.

**Rating update (2026):** Etsy updated how average review ratings are calculated to be more consistent across shops.

### Messaging

**What sellers CAN do:**
- Use Messages to communicate about orders, answer questions, send updates.
- Automated vacation-mode auto-replies are allowed and count for response rate purposes.

**What sellers CANNOT do:**
- Harass, threaten, or discriminate against buyers.
- Use Messages to warn buyers away from other sellers/shops.
- Move buyers off-platform (e.g., request payment outside Etsy).

**Customer Service Standards (minimum):**
- Respond to 80% of first messages within 48 hours.
- Star Seller requires 95% within 24 hours.

## 9. Tax & Payouts

### Sales tax (US)

- Etsy automatically collects and remits US state sales tax on behalf of sellers as a **marketplace facilitator** in all states that require it.
- Sellers do not need to register for state sales tax permits or file state returns for Etsy-facilitated sales.
- Sellers have no opt-out; tax is calculated and remitted entirely by Etsy.

### VAT / GST (international)

- Etsy automatically calculates and remits VAT, GST, or similar taxes for digital items and in countries where Etsy is the deemed supplier (EU, Australia, and others).
- For physical items shipped internationally, customs duties and import taxes may be buyer's responsibility (varies by country/threshold).

### Tax forms (US sellers, 2025 tax year)

- Etsy issues a **1099-K** if gross sales > $20,000 AND > 200 transactions in the calendar year (2025 threshold). State thresholds may be lower.
- Sellers should check their specific state threshold.

### Payout schedule

- Options: **daily, weekly, biweekly, or monthly**. Set in Payment Account settings.
- Weekly/biweekly/monthly disbursements are initiated on Mondays; funds typically credited to bank within 5 business days.
- Daily disbursements triggered each day the balance meets the minimum threshold.
- New sellers: funds typically available for deposit 14-20 days after a sale during initial period.
- Established sellers: may become available the next business day.
- **Instant Transfer** (US only, eff. December 2025): 1% fee (min $0.25, max $1.00); funds settle within ~30 minutes, 24/7.

### Currency

- Sellers set their payout currency.
- If a buyer pays in a different currency, Etsy applies a **2.5% conversion fee**.
- Etsy Payments is the required payment system in most countries where it is available.

### Accepted payment methods (buyers)

Credit/debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Etsy Gift Cards, Etsy Credits, and (as of Oct 2025) **Pay by Bank**.

## 10. Account Suspensions & Appeals

### Suspension types

- **Temporary suspension** — Usually triggered by a policy review, payment hold, or verification requirement. Seller can often resolve by addressing the flagged issue.
- **Permanent suspension** — Account closed for serious or repeated violations.

### Appeals process

1. Seller has **6 months** from suspension date to file an appeal.
2. File via the Help Center: state the reason, describe corrective actions taken, upload supporting documents.
3. A specialist reviews the appeal and account history; response may take up to **2 weeks**.
4. Decision is final — if appeal is denied, no further reinstatement is possible.

### Policy Violations page (new, Fall 2025)

- New dedicated page in Shop Manager showing all actioned listings and policy reasons.
- Listing-level appeals are now available (rolling out gradually from Fall 2025), allowing sellers to request review of listings removed in error.

## 11. Etsy Ads vs Offsite Ads

| | Etsy Ads (onsite) | Offsite Ads |
|---|---|---|
| Where ads appear | Etsy search results, category pages, market pages | Google, Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest, Buzzfeed, and other external sites |
| Pricing model | Cost-per-click (CPC) | Cost-per-sale (charged only when order placed) |
| Cost | ~$0.20-0.50 per click (varies) | 15% of order (< $10k/yr sales) or 12% (>= $10k/yr sales) |
| Enrollment | Opt-in; start/stop any time | All sellers auto-enrolled; shops < $10k/yr can opt out |
| Control | Full control: choose listings, set daily budget | No control over which listings are promoted or budget |
| Attribution window | Per click | 30 days after click |
| Mandatory? | No | Yes for shops with >= $10,000 in sales in prior 365 days |
