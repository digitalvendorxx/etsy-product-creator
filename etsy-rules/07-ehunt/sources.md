## Notes on source reliability

- EHunt blog comparisons (ehunt.ai/blog/*) are self-published and biased toward EHunt. Used only for pricing data cross-reference.
- GoldCityVentures pricing for EHunt (Basic $7.99, Pro $15.99) may be outdated; chrismjackson.com cites different figures ($3.99 basic, $19.99 premium). EHunt pricing page did not load detailed content dynamically.
- All accuracy claims (EHunt 80%, Koalanda 89%/90%, Marmalead 95%) are self-reported by each company. No independent third-party audits found.
- Reddit discussions on sales estimate accuracy did not surface in searches - direct Reddit queries returned no links. The "directionally accurate within 20-30%" figure comes from indirect review site synthesis, not a primary Reddit thread.
- eRank's Aug 2025 API adaptation note sourced from search result snippets, not full article fetch.
