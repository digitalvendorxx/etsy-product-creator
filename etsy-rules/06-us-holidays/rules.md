## 5. POD-Specific Reality — Shipping Constraints

Aysham uses Printonami (POD). Key constraints:

**Production time:** POD orders typically take 2-5 business days to produce before shipping.

**Standard US shipping:** Add 3-7 business days. Total fulfillment from order to delivery: 5-12 business days in normal conditions.

**Holiday surcharges:** Carriers (USPS, UPS, FedEx) add surcharges and experience delays Nov-Dec. Add 2-3 extra days to estimates during peak.

**Christmas hard cutoff:** Orders must be placed by approximately Dec 10-15 for guaranteed standard US delivery by Dec 25. Exact cutoff depends on Printonami's published dates each year — check annually and post in listings by Dec 1.

**Rule of thumb for all events:** Add "order by [date]" language to listing descriptions and shop announcement starting 2 weeks before the event. This reduces INR claims and sets buyer expectations.

**Late decision cost:** A listing created the week before Mother's Day will not have SEO traction. Etsy's algorithm rewards listings that have dwell time and early sales. New listings need 4-6 weeks minimum to gain organic placement.

**Practical implication:** For any ★★★ event, the listing must exist at least 6-8 weeks before the holiday or Aysham will not get meaningful organic traffic. Etsy Ads can partially compensate but at higher CPC cost.

## 6. Off-Season Strategy

"Off-season" for Aysham = periods with no major event in the next 4-6 weeks.

**Typical off-season gaps:**
- Late August (post-BTS, pre-Halloween)
- Late January (post-Valentine's launch, pre-St. Patrick's)
- Mid-November (post-Halloween, pre-peak holiday rush)

**What to do:**

1. **Evergreen designs:** Maintain always-on listings that are not holiday-specific — funny quotes, niche hobbies (nurses, teachers, dog moms, book lovers), aesthetic tees, and occupation shirts. These generate baseline revenue year-round.

2. **Next-event prep:** Use off-season windows to research, design, and list upcoming event items so they have maximum dwell time before the traffic spike.

3. **Shop health:** Refresh photos, update SEO on existing listings, review pricing, and analyze which designs sold best last season.

4. **Trend research:** Run eHunt to identify rising long-tail keywords. Build a pipeline of designs for 90-day lookahead events.

5. **Social content:** Create Reels/TikToks showcasing products — this drives external traffic that reduces dependence on Etsy algorithm.

## 7. Aysham Category Fit — Apparel Themes

### Strong Fit (★★★)
- **Gifting occasions:** Mother's Day, Father's Day, Valentine's Day — buyers are actively looking for apparel as gifts
- **Identity/celebration:** Pride Month, Juneteenth, St. Patrick's Day — buyers want to wear their identity
- **Seasonal casual wear:** Halloween, 4th of July, Christmas — event-specific shirts people actually wear
- **Occupation/niche:** Teacher shirts (year-round + BTS/Teacher Appreciation spike), nurse shirts, dog mom, book lover, plant lady

### Moderate Fit (★★)
- **Family occasions:** Thanksgiving, Easter — family matching sets work; individual designs less so
- **Cultural/federal:** Veterans Day (military niche loyal), Hanukkah (small audience)
- **Sales windows:** Black Friday, Cyber Monday — run a coupon/sale, not a new design push

### Poor Fit (★)
- **No apparel demand:** Presidents Day, Labor Day, Columbus Day — these are day-off holidays, not themed apparel events
- **Too generic:** New Year's (low search volume for shirts specifically; party accessories dominate)

### What to Avoid
- Designs that could be confused with licensed or trademarked IP (character names, sports team logos, movie quotes)
- Over-saturated generic phrases without a differentiating angle (plain "Happy Halloween" with no design hook)
- Designs that require very specific sizing/personalization that POD cannot fulfill efficiently

### Perennial Winners for Aysham
Based on Etsy apparel category analysis (UNVERIFIED against Aysham's own sales data — cross-check with Etsy CSV when available):
- Funny occupation shirts (nurses, teachers, therapists)
- Mom identity shirts (boymom, girl mom, dog mom, plant mom)
- Minimalist quote tees with trending phrases
- Couples/matching sets
- Holiday family matching sets (Christmas pajamas, Thanksgiving, Halloween family)
