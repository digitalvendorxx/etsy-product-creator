- **Cyber Monday 2026 (Nov 30), Cyber Monday 2027 (Nov 29):** https://www.calendar-365.com/holidays/cyber-monday.html
- **Hanukkah 2026 (Dec 4-12), Hanukkah 2027 (Dec 24 - Jan 1):** https://www.chabad.org/holidays/chanukah/article_cdo/aid/671899/jewish/When-Is-Hanukkah-Chanukah-Celebrated-in-2025-2026-2027-2028-2029-and-2030.htm
- **Mother's Day 2026 (May 10), 2027 (May 9):** https://publicholidays.com/mothers-day/
- **Father's Day 2026 (Jun 21), 2027 (Jun 20):** https://publicholidays.com/fathers-day/
- **Teacher Appreciation Week 2026 (May 4-8):** https://www.awarenessdays.com/awareness-days-calendar/teacher-appreciation-week/
- **MLK Day 2026 (Jan 19), 2027 (Jan 18):** https://nationaltoday.com/martin-luther-king-day/ + calculated (3rd Monday January)
- **Presidents Day 2026 (Feb 16), 2027 (Feb 15):** Calculated (3rd Monday February)
- **Memorial Day 2026 (May 25), 2027 (May 31):** Calculated (last Monday May)
- **Labor Day 2026 (Sep 7), 2027 (Sep 6):** Calculated (first Monday September)
- **Columbus Day 2026 (Oct 12), 2027 (Oct 11):** Calculated (second Monday October)

All floating-date holidays were independently verified with Python datetime calculations.
